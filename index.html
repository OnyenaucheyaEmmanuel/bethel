About us

Welcome to TheChurch

Bethel Prayer Fellowship is a Christ-centered, Spirit-led community committed to loving God, loving people, and transforming lives. Our heart is to see people come alive in their faith and discover their purpose in God.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who impact their communities with the gospel and demonstrate God's love in action.

Our Vision

To be a lighthouse of hope, healing, and restoration for all people.

Know more about us

Why Us

Keep Your Faith

Live by Faith

Even when the path ahead is uncertain, we choose to trust in God's promises. Faith reminds us that we are never alone — that God's plan is greater than what we can see. Walk boldly, knowing He is with you every step of the way.

Love one another

FJesus commanded us to love as He has loved us. Let our hearts be open, our hands extended, and our lives reflect the unconditional love of Christ. In a divided world, let the church be united in love.

Be courageous

God did not give us a spirit of fear, but of power, love, and a sound mind. Stand firm in your faith, even when the storm comes. Be bold, be brave — for the Lord your God is with you.

Seal of God

We are marked with a divine purpose, chosen and set apart. The Holy Spirit is our guide, our strength, and our assurance. Walk with confidence, knowing you carry the seal of the living God.
